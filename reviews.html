Reviews

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest is rated lower than the first Pirates movie at 53% on Rotten Tomatoes. However, it is rated higher than the next Pirates movie- At World's End. Despite its lower rating, it won an academy award for best visual effects.
